NickelOS

NickelOS is an Open Source organization dedicated to helping the Open Souce community and helping people get into coding.
We believe that the best way to get into coding is to jump in, but without documentation, it is hard to know how to help. All NickelOS projects are open, documented, and come with a TODO.txt for info on what features you can work on.
Feel free to join us on github to see all the projects, or link to a specific project from this site.
If you have any questions, please email us at info@nickelos.org
